As part of my **Prime Minister's Research Fellowship (PMRF)**, I was required to deliver **50 hours of teaching per year** at nearby government colleges. Below is a summary of my teaching and assistantship experience.

---

## Teaching Assistantship — PMRF Outreach

### 1. Hijli College, Kharagpur
**Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal** | August–November 2022

| Subject | Year | Degree |
|---------|------|--------|
| Climatology | 2nd Year | B.Sc. (Hons.) Geography |
| Remote Sensing and GIS | 3rd Year | B.Sc. (Hons.) Geography |

**Topics Covered:**
- *Climatology*: Composition and layering of the atmosphere, greenhouse effect, ozone layer, monsoon circulation and mechanism with reference to India
- *Remote Sensing and GIS*: Principles of Remote Sensing, sensor resolutions, Indian Remote Sensing applications, GIS data structures, interpolation techniques (IDW), georeferencing, vector/raster operations in QGIS
