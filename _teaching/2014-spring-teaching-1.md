As part of my **Prime Minister's Research Fellowship (PMRF)**, I was required 
to deliver **50 hours of teaching per year** at nearby government colleges. 
Below is a summary of my teaching and assistantship experience.

---

## Teaching Assistantship — PMRF Outreach

### 1. Hijli College, Kharagpur
**Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal** | August to November 2022

| Subject | Year | Degree |
|---------|------|--------|
| Climatology | 2nd Year | B.Sc. (Hons.) Geography |
| Remote Sensing and GIS | 3rd Year | B.Sc. (Hons.) Geography |

**Topics Covered:**
- *Climatology*: Composition and layering of the atmosphere, greenhouse 
  effect, ozone layer, monsoon circulation and mechanism with reference to India
- *Remote Sensing and GIS*: Principles of Remote Sensing, sensor resolutions, 
  Indian Remote Sensing applications, GIS data structures, interpolation 
  techniques (IDW), georeferencing, vector and raster operations in QGIS

---

### 2. Purbanchal College, Silapathar
**Dhemaji, Assam** | October 2023

| Subject | Year | Degree |
|---------|------|--------|
| Remote Sensing and GIS | 2nd and 3rd Year | B.A. (Hons.) Geography |

Introduced students to the fundamentals of satellite remote sensing, covering 
how satellites acquire data, types of sensors, and key satellite missions 
including Sentinel and Landsat. Conducted hands-on sessions in QGIS and 
ArcGIS covering basic GIS operations, spatial analysis, and georeferencing. 
Also introduced students to Python libraries for satellite image analysis, 
including reading, visualising and processing raster data.

---

### 3. Kharagpur College, Kharagpur
**Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal** | October 2023

| Subject | Year | Degree |
|---------|------|--------|
| Remote Sensing and GIS | 3rd Year | B.A. (Hons.) Geography |

Covered the basics of satellite remote sensing and GIS, with a focus on 
understanding spatial data, coordinate systems, and map projections. 
Introduced students to GIS-based analysis workflows using QGIS and ArcGIS, 
and demonstrated how satellite imagery from Sentinel and Landsat can be 
used for land cover and environmental analysis.

---

### 4. Kharagpur College, Kharagpur
**Paschim Medinipur, West Bengal** | November 2023 to July 2024

| Subject | Year | Degree |
|---------|------|--------|
| Remote Sensing and GIS | 3rd Year | B.A. (Hons.) Geography |
| Disaster Management | 3rd Year | B.A. (Hons.) Geography |

*Remote Sensing and GIS:* Delivered a full course covering remote sensing 
principles, GIS data structures, and practical analysis in QGIS and ArcGIS. 
Practical sessions included georeferencing, vector and raster operations, 
and an introduction to Python-based satellite image processing using 
Sentinel and Landsat datasets.

*Disaster Management:* Taught students about natural hazards and their 
causes, impacts, and mitigation. Topics included heatwaves and their 
connection to large-scale atmospheric circulation, tropical cyclones and 
storm surge dynamics, tsunamis and their triggering mechanisms, and 
earthquake hazards including seismic zonation and vulnerability. Classes 
combined theory with real-world case studies from the Indian subcontinent.
